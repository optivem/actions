- [ ] **Replace flat-interval retries in `wait-for-urls`, `build-docker-image`, `push-docker-image` with exponential/jittered backoff** — each of these uses a fixed `sleep N` between attempts. Rubric §1.3 bounded-retry rule requires both a ceiling (present) and exponential-or-jittered growth (missing). During a partial outage a fixed 10s/15s loop hammers the downstream at the exact cadence most likely to exacerbate a cascade. Switch to `sleep $((base * 2 ** (attempt - 1)))` with optional jitter.
